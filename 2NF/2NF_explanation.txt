2NF Stage Explanation — Group 9
================================

WHAT IS 2NF?
------------
A table is in Second Normal Form when:
  1. It is already in 1NF.
  2. Every non-key attribute depends on the whole composite key,
     not just part of it (no partial dependencies).


1. TABLES WITH COMPOSITE KEYS
---------------------------
After 1NF, the following tables had composite primary keys and were
checked for partial dependencies:

  Project_Supplier_Material(ProjectID, SupplierName, MaterialSupplied)
  Project_Equipment(ProjectID, EquipmentUsed)
  Project_Worker(ProjectID, WorkerName)
  Worker_Skills(WorkerName, Skill)
  Worker_Certifications(WorkerName, Certification)
  Supplier_Phones(SupplierName, SupplierPhone)


PARTIAL DEPENDENCIES FOUND
---------------------------

1. MATERIAL UNIT COST (Project_Supplier_Material → MaterialUnitCost)

   In the 1NF table Project_Supplier_Material:
     (ProjectID, SupplierName, MaterialSupplied) → MaterialUnitCost

   MaterialUnitCost depends only on (SupplierName, MaterialSupplied),
   not on ProjectID. This is a partial dependency.

   Fix: Moved MaterialUnitCost into a new Supplier_Material table.
        Project_Supplier_Material becomes a pure junction table.

   New tables:
     Supplier_Material(SupplierName, MaterialSupplied, MaterialUnitCost)
     Project_Supplier_Material(ProjectID, SupplierName, MaterialSupplied)


2. EQUIPMENT RENTAL COST (Project_Equipment → EquipmentRentalCost)

   In the 1NF table Project_Equipment:
     (ProjectID, EquipmentUsed) → EquipmentRentalCost

   EquipmentRentalCost depends only on EquipmentUsed, not on ProjectID.
   This is a partial dependency.

   Fix: Moved EquipmentRentalCost into a new Equipment table.
        Project_Equipment becomes a pure junction table.

   New tables:
     Equipment(EquipmentUsed, EquipmentRentalCost)
     Project_Equipment(ProjectID, EquipmentUsed)


3. PRIMARY KEYS AND FOREIGN KEYS INTRODUCED IN 2NF
------------------------------------------------
  New primary keys:
    Supplier_Material  → PK: (SupplierName, MaterialSupplied)
    Equipment          → PK: EquipmentUsed

  New foreign keys:
    Project_Supplier_Material.(SupplierName, MaterialSupplied) → Supplier_Material
    Project_Equipment.EquipmentUsed                            → Equipment


4. TABLES CARRIED FORWARD FROM 1NF (UNCHANGED STRUCTURE)
------------------------------------------------------
  Supplier(SupplierName, SupplierCity)
  Supplier_Phones(SupplierName, SupplierPhone)
  worker(WorkerName, WorkerPhone, WorkerHourlyRate)
  Worker_Skills(WorkerName, Skill)
  Worker_Certifications(WorkerName, Certification)
  Project_Worker(ProjectID, WorkerName)

  These tables either have single-column primary keys (partial
  dependencies are impossible) or are pure junction tables with no
  non-key attributes.


5. WHY THE DESIGN IS NOW IN 2NF
-----------------------------
Every non-key attribute in every table depends on the full primary key.
MaterialUnitCost now lives in Supplier_Material where the full key is
(SupplierName, MaterialSupplied). EquipmentRentalCost now lives in
Equipment where the full key is EquipmentUsed. Junction tables carry
only foreign keys and no partially-dependent attributes.
